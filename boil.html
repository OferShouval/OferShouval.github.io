Video Filters

A series of video filters, developed in Processing. They process video files or a webcam feed in real time and can thus be used to create interesting stage videos or even music videos - see the example below. I've put together three iterations so far, but I plan on creating more, and possibly even developing plug ins that can be opened in programs such as After Effects.
